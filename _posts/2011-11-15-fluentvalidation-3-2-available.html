--- layout: post title: "FluentValidation 3.2 Available" ---

FluentValidation 3.2 is now available for download.

This is a maintenance release that addresses a couple of bugs from FluentValidation 3.1:

  • The NotEmptyValidator can now be used with collections that don't implement ICollection (such as Entity Framework's EntityCollection<T>)
  • AbstractValidator.Custom can now be used within RuleSets.
